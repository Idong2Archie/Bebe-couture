Frequently Asked Questions

How many persons can I bring along to view your gowns?

You may bring along two persons to view and try on our gowns. Appointments are for one hour long with a charge of $60.00 consultation fee.

Can I customise or alter any of your designs?

Yes. We have lots of brides that want to customise and alter our designs. This is done in our Auckland studio with talented consultants. They will give you a price interaction for customisation of the gown.

I am hoping to lose a bit of weight before my special day, if I order now will my dress still fit me?

Yes. We have lots of brides hoping to lose weight before their special day, which is no problem at all. If you drop a size, our dresses can be easily be altered by our team of professional seamstresses.

Will my dress be perfect?

All of our dresses undergo a final 4-step high-grade professional quality control to ensure fabrics and manufacturing is of the highest standard before delivery. Our team is highly qualified and experienced, and we pride ourselves of our premium reputation. Our brides are always very happy with their gowns. You can check our collection to view our real brides.

Can I be assured of your business?

Our reputation is exceptional. We work with brides in New Zealand and Australia and pride ourselves of delivering the best service with the best product, and with the most beautiful designs. Our designers and team have over 20 years experience in design and manufacturing. With our skills and vast experience, our team can deliver the best results.

Where are your dresses made?

Our beautiful dresses are handmade in our production studio in Auckland, New Zealand, by our in-house team of professional seamstresses.

Do you make custom dresses?

Yes, we do. Make an appointment on our website to meet with our senior consultants for an hour.

How do I take my measurements to purchase a gown?

If you can’t come to our Auckland Studio for fitting and you would like to clarify your size and how to take your measurements, we will email you guides and sizing charts to help determine this.

How do I place my order?

It is all very easy, simply make your dress choice and place your order through our website and enter all your measurements into the notes section. Our team of professional ladies are online and on the phone all day to assist you.

Kenny & Harlow is the bridal wear range of Designers that offers a range of exquisite collections and bespoke designs for all types of brides.
